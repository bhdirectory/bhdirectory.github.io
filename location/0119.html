Description

Room 19 is located on the first floor of the House, along the link corridor between the House and Annexe.
It is a large nursing home room with ensuite shower room
It is south-facing with a view over the main lawn, parkland and lake
The room was refurbished in 2017 and is in excellent condition with new furniture.
It has a double-glazed window and a ceiling hoist.

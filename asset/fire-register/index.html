Fire Safety Arrangements

A substantial upgrade to the automatic fire detection system took place in 2016 making the system fully addressable and compliant to the appropriate L1 standard. A comprehensive Fire Risk Assessment has been carried out by a third-party. Recommendations have been actioned according to an Fire Safety Improvement Plan. There is a service agreement in place with Elite Fire Ltd.

The company has a Fire Safety Policy and Fire Action and Evacuation Procedures have been drawn up. All staff undergo an annual Fire Awareness Training. Staff with additional responsibilities have further training, including use of emergency evacuation equipment.

Emergency Lighting provides an alternative source of lighting along all escape routes in the event of a power failure. The units are visually inspected every day as part of the Daily Maintenance Check. The units undergo a function test every month to ensure that they are operational. An annual drop test checks for a fully functional battery to ensure that the unit will remain powered for a minimum of three hours in the event of a power failure.

This checklist covers the minimum standard of checks required to ensure the safe operation of each piece of equipment in operation. The check should be completed on a frequent basis.

  1. Inspect equipment for cleanliness
  2. Inspect equipment frame for damage
  3. Check all fittings for security
  4. Ensure all protective coverings and caps are fitted
  5. Ensure the equipment folds and unfolds easily
    6. (where applicable)
  6. Check wheels for any damage or wear
  7. Check brake assemblies for correct operation
  8. Examine tyres for damage and wear
    9. (where applicable)
  9. Check tyre pressures
    10. (where applicable)
  10. Check that all footplates/leg-rests are securely fitted
    11. (where applicable)
  11. Examine footplate treads for damage or wear
    12. (where applicable)
  12. Examine all upholstery elements for damage or wear
  13. Examine belts for correct operation, damage or wear
    14. (where applicable)
  14. Is the equipment correctly labelled? (refer to Mobility Equipment Register for details)
  15. Are there any other issues that need reporting?
  16. Please rate the condition of the equipment (A: GOOD, B: FAIR or C: POOR)

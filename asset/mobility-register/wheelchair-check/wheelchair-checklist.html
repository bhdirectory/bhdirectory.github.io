Wheelchair Checklist

This checklist covers the minimum standard of checks required to ensure the safe operation of each wheelchair in operation. The check should be completed for each wheelchair each month.

  1. Inspect wheelchair for cleanliness
  2. Inspect wheelchair frame for damage
  3. Check all fittings for security
  4. Ensure all protective coverings and caps are fitted
  5. Ensure the chair folds and unfolds easily
  6. Check wheels for any damage or wear
  7. Check brake assemblies for correct operation
  8. Examine tyres for damage and wear
  9. Check tyre pressures
  10. Check that all footplates/leg-rests are securely fitted
  11. Examine footplate treads for damage or wear
  12. Examine all upholstery elements for damage or wear
  13. Examine lap belt for correct operation, damage or wear
  14. Is the wheelchair correctly labelled? (refer to Mobility Register for details)
  15. Are there any other issues that need reporting?
  16. Please rate the condition of the wheelchair (A: GOOD, B: FAIR or C: POOR)

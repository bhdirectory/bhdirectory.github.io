Safe Use of Mobility Equipment Arrangements

The company will provide suitable and sufficient mobility equipment for all residents according to their needs. In many cases, residents will provide their own wheelchairs and/or other mobility equipment. A register of wheelchairs is maintained and updated regularly. Ad hoc maintenance is provided by our own staff. Where repairs require specialist parts or are not straightforward, a third-party specialist will be contracted. A proactive monthly check of all wheelchairs for cleanliness and function has been devised. All electric-powered wheelchairs are required to have an annual service and inspection and residents are required to have this in place as part of an separate agreement.

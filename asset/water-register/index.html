A comprehensive Water Risk Assessment is completed every two years by an third-party specialist. this identifies and assesses sources of risk from legionella bacteria and recommends suitable controls. The recommendations from this assessment are actioned as far as practicable. We are also managing the risk of scalding from hot water to residents of the Home and have comprehensive technical controls in place to control water temperatures from hot water outlets that are accessible to vulnerable adults.

The person responsible for water safety in the Home is: Tim Whalley, who has had suitable training in the Control of Legionella in Hot and Cold Water Systems.

Water Services Register

A comprehensive Water Risk Assessment is completed every two years by an third-party specialist. this identifies and assesses sources of risk from legionella bacteria and recommends suitable controls. The recommendations from this assessment are actioned as far as practicable. A schematic of the water systems in the Home is available. Tim Whalley is responsible for the implementation of these arrangements. We are also managing the risk of scalding from hot water to residents of the Home and have comprehensive technical controls in place to control water temperatures from hot water outlets that are accessible to vulnerable adults.

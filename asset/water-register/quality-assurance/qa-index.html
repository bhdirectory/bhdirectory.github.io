TMV Checks (Monthly)

A monthly check of all residential water outlets ensures that the risks of:

  1. Scalding;
  2. Legionella

Engineering controls should be provided to ensure that water hotter than 44 °C is not discharged from outlets that may be accessible to vulnerable people. Hot water temperatures at a thermostatic mixing valves (TMVs) are tested every month to ensure the hot water temperature is at least 50°C at the point it enters the valve is an important legionella risk management task set out in the Health & Safety Executive’s ACOP L8 and HSG274 guidance.

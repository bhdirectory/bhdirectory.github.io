Hoist Checklist

This checklist covers the minimum standard of checks required to ensure the safe and efficient operation of each hoist in operation. The check should be completed for each hoist each month.

  1. Inspect hoist for cleanliness
  2. Inspect hoist for damage or missing parts
  3. Ensure the hoist operates on all handset functions
  4. Check castors for any damage or wear (applicable to mobile hoists)
  5. Check the castor brakes are secure and operate correctly (applicable to mobile hoists)
  6. Check the handset control and cable for damage
  7. Check all actuators for damage and ensure smooth, quiet operation
  8. Check all cables for damage and ensure there is no risk of entanglement
  9. Is the hoist correctly labelled? (refer to LOLER Register for details)
  10. Ensure that hoists are left on their charging cradle (applicable to overhead hoists)
  11. Ensure that all interchangeable batteries compatible with the hoist are accounted for and left on charge (applicable to mobile hoists and Voyager hoist)
  12. Are there any other issues that need reporting?
  13. Please rate the condition of the hoist (A: GOOD, B: FAIR or C: POOR)

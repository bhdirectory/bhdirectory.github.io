Arrangements for Safe Lifting Operations and Lifting Equipment (LOLER)

There are 19 hoists and 3 lifting bath chairs which come under LOLER regulations. Of the 19 hoists, 9 are mobile and 10 are fixed overhead ceiling hoists. Of the 9 mobile hoists, 2 are standing aids. All hoists are maintained over and above the recommended minimum standard. An annual service (which also includes a LOLER test) is supplemented by two further LOLER examinations visits from an independent engineer arranged as part of a comprehensive Engineering Insurance Policy which covers all patient lifting equipment. In addition to the 'pre-start' checks that all staff are asked to complete before each lifting operation, a monthly check by our own Maintenance Team ensures that the hoists are maintained in a safe and effective manner. The company has conducted a risk assessment with regard to manual handling operations undertaken by staff on residents.

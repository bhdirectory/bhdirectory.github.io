The process to have devices serviced on an IPM is as follows:

  1. Each home to contact BD (CME) UK Limited on 01253 206700 or email BD, quote your contract reference number and the serial no of the device to be returned.
  2. Include a completed decontamination form and a returns form in the parcel (BD (CME) Uk Limited to supply these);
  3. Return to BD (CME) UK;
  4. Device Serviced and/or repaired;
  5. Device returned to your home.

The company has a Working at Height Policy and risk assessment. Only staff who have undergone ladder training should use a ladder. The need for the use of a ladder should be determined by a risk assessment, although for straightforward daily tasks such a changing a lightbulb, hanging a picture or storing something on a high shelf, such an assessment will be done at the time by the individual using the ladder or step-ladder. Maintenance, Housekeeping and Kitchen staff undergo suitable training and have access to ladders and step-ladders. For all work involving platforms, trestles or scaffolding, RAMS will be provided before work starts. This applies to contractors working on site as well.

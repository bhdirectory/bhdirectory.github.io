Provision and Use of Work Equipment (PUWER)

We will ensure that all work equipment is suitable for purpose and maintained in good working order. We will ensure that all work equipment is correctly installed and inspected at suitable intevals. Where appropriate, due to specific risks that have been identified, we will ensure that the equipment is only used, repaired or maintained by suitably competent persons. Suitable health and safety information and training will be provided to employees who operate the equipment and access to the equipment or locations where the equipment is stored is restricted to authroised persons only. Control measures will be provided according to manufacturers recommendations or specific risk asssessment.

Gas Safety

We will ensure that all gas appliances, installations and pipework or flues installed at the premises are maintained in a safe condition and work on this equipment is only undertaken by suitably qualified thrid-parties registered with the Council for Regsitered Gas Installers (CORGI).

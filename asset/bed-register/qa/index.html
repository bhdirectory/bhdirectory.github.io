From April 2019, all Electric Profiling Beds will be checked each month for cleanliness, safety and effective function. The checklist specifies what should be inspected by our Maintenance & Housekeeping Teams. The need for an annual service by a third-party specialist is under consideration. Related checks are carried out by our Clinical Auditor who assesses the condition of mattresses and bedrails in more detail. These paper-based records are available through the Home Manager.

Electrical Distribution

We will ensure that all electrical systems are maintained in a safe condition. All work on these systems will be carried out by done by competent persons using safe systems of work. Live work will only be done in extremely limited circumstances with prior approval of our insurers. The fixed mains installation is inspected and tested periodically by a competent person at the appropriate frequency in accordance with the current regulations. Copies of inspection reports can be found on the data page for each distribution board. A Periodic Inspection Schedule provides the most up-to-date summary of inspections that are due.

Portable Appliances

An annual inspection of portable appliances is carried out by a competant third-party contractor who has been providing the service for over 20 years. This continuity is familiarity with the site and our residents and staff ensures that a comprehensive test is carried out as efficiently as possible whilst causing minimal disturbance to residents.

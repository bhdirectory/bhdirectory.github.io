Electrical Safety Arrangements

We will ensure that all electrical systems are maintained in a safe condition. All work on these systems will be carried out by done by competant persons using safe systems of work. Live work will only be done in extremely limited circumstances with prior approval of our insurers. The fixed mains installation is inspected and tested periodically by a competent person at the appropriate frequency in accordance with the current regulations. Copies of inspection reports can be found on the data page for each distribution board. A Periodic Inspection Schedule provides the most up-to-date summary of inspections that are due.

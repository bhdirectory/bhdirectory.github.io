Safety Check Register

A Daily Maintenance Check is divided into 3 separate checklists covering Fire Safety, Safety & Security and Heating and Water. It is completed every weekday in line with the normal working hours of the Maintenance Team. In addition, the Team will report any outstanding requests from the Maintenance Request Book which is located in the Nursing Office.

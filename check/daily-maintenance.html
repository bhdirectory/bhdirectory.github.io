Daily Maintenance Check

Safety & Security Check
checklist details

Ensure the following:

  1. the generator compound is secure and fuel level is adequate;
  2. the external fire escape ramp is clear from obstruction and conditions underfoot are safe;
  3. there is no excessive build up of waste outside the kitchen exit door or in the maintenance yard;
  4. all locked doors to restricted areas are secure;
    restricted areas
    1. Maintenance Workshop;
    2. Mews Plant Room;
    3. Mews Lift Motor Room;
    4. Main Plant Room;
    5. Basement;
    6. Annexe Lift Motor Room;
    7. Woodchip Boiler Room;
    8. Maintenance Storage Containers;
  5. there is no excessive storage of flammable materials in any plant rooms;
 		SATISFACTORY REPORT ISSUE(S)
Heating & Water Check
checklist details

Ensure the following:

  1. the domestic hot water flow on the Main Panel is ≥ 65°C;
  2. all heating return readings on the Main Panel are ≥ 50°C;
  3. the Mikrofill pressure reading in the Main Plant Room is ≥ 1.4 Bar;
  4. the domestic hot water reading on the Mews boiler is ≥ 60°C;
  5. the pressure reading in the Mews Plant Room is ≥ 1.4 Bar;
  6. that there are no faults on either heating systems;
 		SATISFACTORY REPORT ISSUE(S)
Fire Safety Check
checklist details

Ensure the following:

  1. all fire extinguishers are correctly located;
  2. all emergency lighting has been visually checked;
  3. fire escape routes are clear of obstructions;
  4. there are no faults on the fire panel;
  5. there are no hot works expected today (if so, please report);
 		SATISFACTORY REPORT ISSUE(S)

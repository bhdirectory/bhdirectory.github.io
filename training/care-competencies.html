Care Competencies

The Company has developed a series of optional training modules for staff to demonstrate an in-depth knowledge of some of the higher risk elements of the care role and how they relate to the specific arrangements in place at Birtley House.

By successfully completing an internal assessment for each competency, staff are able to show a commitment to their role and good understanding of the operational side of the Nursing Home which will be required in order to achieve promotion.

Where specific interests are identified, staff will be encouraged to build upon the Competency by developing a project which would achieve an improvement in working practice or an enhancement for the quality fo life of the residents. Successful completion of at least one approved project would be required in order to become a Lead Carer.

CC01 | Mobility
  1. Wheelchairs & mobility scooters
  2. Non-lifting transfers
  3. Disabled vehicle & escorts

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to supervise an escorted journey for a resident using a wheelchair.

CC02 | Confidentiality & the sharing of healthcare information
  1. Lifecycle of a record
  2. When to share confidential personal information
  3. Writing care records

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to attend and contribute to a care plan review meeting.

CC03 | Beds, bed rails & mattresses
  1. The Electric Profiling Bed (EPB)
  2. Bed rails, restrictions & DoLS
  3. Preventing pressure sores

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to shadow the Clinical Auditor during a bed & mattress audit.

CC04 | Lifting operations
  1. Hoist & slings
  2. Battery management
  3. LOLER regulations

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to conduct a monthly hoist check as per the relevant procedure.

CC05 | Good housekeeping
  1. Residential spaces
  2. Laundry operations
  3. Legionella control & water safety
  4. Clinical waste

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to complete a shadow-shift with a member of the Housekeeping Team.

CC06 | Emergency response
  1. Personal Emergency Evacuation Plans (PEEPs)
  2. Strategy & drills
  3. Evacuation equipment
  4. Business continuity

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to plan and conduct an evacuation drill involving vertical evacuation.

CC07 | Assistive technologies
  1. Nursecall system
  2. Environmental controls
  3. Monitoring & restrictions

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to set up a floor sensor mat (or other accessory) connected to a SensorMate device.

CC08 | History & ethos
  1. Birtley House
  2. Family ethos
  3. Community engagement

In addition to passing the modules, the staff member would be required to conduct a 'tour' of the building with a resident.

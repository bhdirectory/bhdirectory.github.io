Description

Correct waste segregation ensures that each waste stream is dealt with appropriately, reducing the risk of injury or infection as well as reducing the costs of waste disposal. For each type of waste, appropriate and sufficient containers are used to store the waste until collection by a licenced third-party or other suitable disposal method.

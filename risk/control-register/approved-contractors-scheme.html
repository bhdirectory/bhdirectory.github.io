Approved Contractors Scheme

Control ID 11301
Control Icon
Description

In addition to the necessary supervision required to fulfil HSE recommendations, the company has responsibility for ensuring that third-party service providers comply with regulations set out in the Health and Social Care Act 2008 (Regulated Activities) Regulations 2014.

Contractors must be registered onto the Company's Approved Contractor Scheme which provides relevant H&S information, site rules and ensures that third-parties are suitable to work within our regulated environment.

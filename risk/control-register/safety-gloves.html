Safety Gloves

Hazard ID 11206
Control Icon
Description

Safety gloves can protect the hands from a variety of risks including abrasion, temperature extremes, cuts and punctures, impact, chemicals, electric shock, skin infection, disease or contamination. The correct type of safety glove should be chosen to suit the task.

We invite and welcome feedback as a means of improving the service that we offer, this includes any concerns or complaints. A suggestion box can be found in the main reception hall and we welcome ideas, comments and suggestions from all, whether that is as a Resident, residents’ family, staff, visitors and volunteers.

If you have a concern or complaint, we want to resolve it, quickly and simply. If you are unhappy about any aspect of the service, please bring it to our attention immediately. By talking to the member of staff you usually deal with, their manager or the Registered Manager, issues can often be resolved quickly.

If you feel it necessary to escalate your complaint at any stage, please write to: Tim Whalley, Birtley House Nursing Home, Birtley Road, Bramley, Surrey GU5 0LB. A written acknowledgement will be sent within seven days. You also have the right to take the complaint externally: Please see ‘Regulating Body’ for contact details.

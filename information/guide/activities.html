Our Activities Programme

At Birtley we pride ourselves on maintaining a busy, varied and interesting programme throughout the year, full of imagination and thought.

The aim is to attract as many residents as possible to join in the structured activities or outings which take place five times a week. The Activities team are available throughout the day thus providing the opportunity for residents to discuss any personal problems or requirements.

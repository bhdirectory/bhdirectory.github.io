Birtley House Group Limited

Birtley House Group Ltd. was set up in 2008 as part of a re-structuring of a family business that consisted of Eyhurst Court Limited, Birtley Mews Limited, Birtley Brook Estates Limited (now and LLP) and Historic House Retirement Homes Limited. Birtley House Group (BHG) is the parent organisation for the remaining subsidiaries which all remain under the ownership of the Whalley family.
